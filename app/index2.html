It all began about 2 years ago at the W1SE Castle Community in Solana Beach, CA. Several friends chose co-working and co-living to better manage life during covid, to support each other, to help each other, to become better and stronger, to go through those difficult times. Because we gave up bad habits and drove each other to develop, mentally, spiritually, and physically, we began to attract the most amazing people into our life. People who intend to grow.
